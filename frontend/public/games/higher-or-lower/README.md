# Higher or Lower

Guess whether the next card will be higher or lower than the current one. Chain correct guesses to build your streak and multiplier.

## Controls
- **Arrow Up / Higher**: Guess next card is higher or equal.
- **Arrow Down / Lower**: Guess next card is lower or equal.
- **Double Or Nothing**: Risk your current turn's points for double reward.
- **Hint**: Reveal the color and range (High/Low) of the next card once per run.

## Scoring
- Base points: 10
- Streak Multiplier: Increases by 0.2x for each consecutive correct guess.
- Daily Mode: Everyone plays the same shuffled deck.
