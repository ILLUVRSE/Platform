# Wheel of Fortune

Spin the wheel, guess the letters, and solve the puzzle!

## Controls
- **Spin:** Click the wheel or press ENTER.
- **Guess Letter:** Click the on-screen keyboard or press keys A-Z.
- **Buy Vowel:** Click 'Buy Vowel' or click a Vowel button (cost: $250).
- **Solve:** Click 'SOLVE' to type the answer.

## Rules
- Solve 5 puzzles to complete the run.
- Avoid 'Bankrupt' (loses round cash) and 'Lose Turn'.
- Vowels cost money and must be bought.
- Score is total accumulated cash across all rounds.
